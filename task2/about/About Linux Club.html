About the Linux Club

The Linux Club was founded in 2023 to bring together open-source enthusiasts and promote Linux adoption and education.

Our Mission

To explore, develop, and celebrate open-source software and Linux systems.

What We Do

  • Organize monthly workshops
  • Host coding sessions
  • Contribute to open-source projects

How to Join

  1. Fill out the application form
  2. Attend a new member orientation
  3. Start contributing to projects
